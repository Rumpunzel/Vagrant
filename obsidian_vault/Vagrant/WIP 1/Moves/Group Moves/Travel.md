Effect : Wits

Roll Supplies

**On a strong hit,** mark progress based on effect and choose 1 (on a critical hit choose 2):
- mark +1 progress
- gain +1 Supplies

**On a weak hit,** mark progress based on effect, lose Supplies equal to inverse effect
